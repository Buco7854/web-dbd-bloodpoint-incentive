Bloodpoint Incentives is a free, unofficial fan dashboard for Dead by Daylight that tracks the live Bloodpoint bonus in every matchmaking region. For each region it shows the current survivor and killer incentive multipliers in real time, highlights the region closest to you, charts how the bonuses have shifted over recent hours and days, and forecasts where they are likely to head next. Readings are gathered by community-run agents and updated continuously. This is a non-commercial project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Behaviour Interactive.
